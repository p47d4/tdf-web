Introduction

The Dada Foundation is a humanitarian non-governmental organization committed to improving the lives of vulnerable children and families in need. We focus on providing essential resources and support to enhance the well-being of those in underserved communities. As part of our ongoing commitment to social impact, we propose the implementation of a pilot program, the "Orphanage Nutritious Feeding Program," aimed at addressing food insecurity among children in orphanages.

  • Program Name: Girei Orphange Nutritious Feeding Program
  • Location: Girei, Adamawa
  • Date of Launch: May 24, 2024
  • Program url: www.thedadafoundation.org.ng/onfp-girei

More On ONFP Girei

Nourishing the Future: Girei's Orphanage Feeding Program Calls to Action

Across Northern Nigeria, orphanages face a heartbreaking reality: providing adequate and nutritious meals to their children proves a constant struggle. Limited funding, disrupted supply chains, and restricted access to essential foods create a situation where malnutrition threatens the well-being of these vulnerable children. This malnutrition can have lasting consequences, hindering their physical and cognitive development and impacting their health for years to come.

Girei's Orphanage Nutritious Feeding Program is rising to meet this challenge head-on. Our program is built on several key objectives:

  1. One Nutritious Meal, Every Day: Our primary goal is to ensure every child residing in Girei's orphanages receives at least one nutritious meal each day. This will provide them with the essential building blocks they need to grow and develop into healthy young adults.
  2. Building a Healthier Future: By establishing a strong nutritional foundation, we aim to improve the overall health and well-being of Girei's orphaned children. This includes strengthening their immune systems, increasing their energy levels, and reducing their risk of chronic diseases later in life.
  3. Stronger Communities Through Collaboration: The program fosters partnerships between orphanages and local Girei food suppliers. This strengthens the local economy and creates a collaborative environment where everyone plays a role in supporting our most vulnerable community members.
  4. A Scalable Model for Lasting Change: Girei's program is designed to be a model for success. By demonstrating its effectiveness, we hope to inspire the creation of similar programs in other regions across Nigeria, ensuring all orphaned children have the chance to thrive.

We believe that every child deserves the opportunity to reach their full potential. Girei's Orphanage Nutritious Feeding Program is a critical step towards achieving that goal. With your support, we can ensure Girei's most vulnerable children have access to the food they need to lead healthy, productive lives.

Program Objectives

The primary objectives of the Orphanage Nutritious Feeding Program are: To ensure that children in orphanages receive at least one nutritious meal per day. To improve the overall health and well-being of orphaned children. To foster stronger partnerships between orphanages and local food suppliers. To create a scalable model that can be replicated in other regions.
